# AceView - Demo Script for Judges

## 🎬 2-Minute Demo Script

Use this script for hackathon presentations and judge evaluations.

---

## Setup (Before Demo)

```bash
cd frontend
npm run dev
```

Open http://localhost:3000 in browser  
Have Dev Debug Panel ready to click

---

## Script

### Opening (15 seconds)

> "Hi judges! I'm excited to show you **AceView** - an AI Interview Coach that provides real-time feedback on body language and speech patterns.
>
> The problem we're solving: 73% of qualified candidates fail interviews due to poor presentation, not lack of skills. AceView helps them practice and improve with instant AI feedback."

**Action**: Show home page, point to feature cards

---

### Feature #1: Confidence Ring (30 seconds)

> "Let me show you our first judge magnet feature - the **Confidence Ring**.
>
> This dynamic glowing border around the video changes color based on your overall confidence level. Right now it's green because I'm doing well."

**Action**: Click "Start Practice Session", point to green ring

> "But watch what happens when my posture drops..."

**Action**: Click "Trigger Slouch" in Dev Debug Panel

> "The ring instantly turns red, giving immediate visual feedback. This helps users correct their posture in real-time during practice."

**Action**: Point to red ring and LOW indicator

---

### Feature #2: Filler Word Highlighter (30 seconds)

> "Our second feature is the **Live Filler Word Highlighter**.
>
> As you speak, the transcript automatically detects and highlights filler words like 'umm', 'uh', and 'like' in red."

**Action**: Point to transcript panel

> "Let me trigger some filler words..."

**Action**: Click "Trigger Filler Word" 2-3 times

> "See how they're instantly highlighted? The counter also tracks how many you've used. This helps users become aware of their speech patterns and reduce filler words over time."

**Action**: Point to highlighted words and counter

---

### Feature #3: Invisible AI Nudges (30 seconds)

> "The third feature is **Invisible AI Nudges** - smart suggestions that appear without being intrusive.
>
> These minimalist overlays provide helpful tips during your practice session."

**Action**: Click "Trigger AI Nudge"

> "Notice how it appears in the top-right corner with a subtle design, then auto-dismisses after 5 seconds. This makes it perfect for real interviews too - you can have it running on a second screen without the interviewer noticing."

**Action**: Point to nudge, wait for auto-dismiss

---

### Technical Excellence (15 seconds)

> "Technically, we built this with **Next.js 15** and **TypeScript** for type safety, **Tailwind CSS** for the futuristic dark mode aesthetic, and **Zustand** for efficient state management.
>
> The frontend is ready to integrate with the Vision Agents SDK backend for real-time video analysis using YOLO, Deepgram, and Gemini."

**Action**: Briefly show metrics panel

---

### Dashboard (15 seconds)

> "We also have a dashboard that tracks your progress over time..."

**Action**: Navigate to dashboard

> "You can see session history, improvement trends, and personalized insights on what to work on."

**Action**: Point to stats and insights panels

---

### Closing (15 seconds)

> "To recap: AceView provides **real-time AI coaching** with three judge magnet features:
> 1. Confidence Ring for instant visual feedback
> 2. Filler Word Highlighter for speech improvement
> 3. Invisible AI Nudges for smart suggestions
>
> All wrapped in a futuristic dark mode design that's ready for production. Thank you!"

**Action**: Return to home page or interview session

### Common Questions

**Q: Is this using real AI analysis?**
> "Currently it's in mock mode for the demo, but the frontend is architected to integrate with Vision Agents SDK, YOLO for pose detection, Deepgram for speech-to-text, and Gemini for answer analysis."

**Q: How does the confidence ring work?**
> "It calculates an overall confidence score from three metrics: posture, eye contact, and speech pace. The ring color changes based on that score - green for high, yellow for medium, red for low."

**Q: Can this be used during real interviews?**
> "Yes! The Invisible AI Nudges feature is designed for that. You can run it on a second screen or phone, and it provides subtle text suggestions that won't be detected by the interviewer."

**Q: What's the business model?**
> "Freemium model: 3 free practice sessions, then $9.99/month for unlimited access. We're also targeting universities at $4.99 per student per year for bulk licenses."

**Q: What's next after the hackathon?**
> "We're planning to integrate the Vision Agents SDK backend, add user authentication, implement video recording and replay, and launch a beta with college students."
